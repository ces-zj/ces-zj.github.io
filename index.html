Introduction

Although not obvious as much, there are known ethic rules for web design that should be followed. All the factors of web design is based on the satisfaction of the people or the "end user". It is our responsabilty as the web designers to carefully choose our design and how it would impact the end reader. By making choices that are ethical and user-centered, we create a better web for everyone.
